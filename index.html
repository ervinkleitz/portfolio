About Me

I am Ervin Kleitz Gonzales, a San Francisco-based Engineering grad, Full Stack Developer, Entrepreneur, and Fooddie. I create clean and easy-to-use web apps.

My first dabblings with code (aside from the ones normally in school) began with me tinkering around with bits and pieces of MySpace and Friendster code. It felt empowering and liberating being able to manipulate code and see the results actually take shape before my eyes. I eventually began a love affair with coding, especially with JavaScript, as I realized that this was something I would love to be doing for life.

When not coding, I spend my free time with family, playing MMORPGs, watching Anime (yep, I am a geek), running, and, trying out new food.
