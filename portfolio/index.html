About Me

I am Ervin Kleitz Gonzales, a San Francisco-based doulbe major Engineering grad, Full Stack Developer, Entrepreneur, and Fooddie. I create clean and easy-to-use web applications.

I already had technical career of sorts before going into coding full-time. I spent some time with a popular Semiconductor company, a Telecommunications firm, and a contact center until I founded my own company. It was during that time that I got re-acquianted with coding and rekindled my passion for it.

My first dabblings with code (aside from the ones I had in school) began with me tinkering around with bits and pieces of MySpace and Friendster code. It felt empowering and liberating being able to manipulate code and see the results actually take shape before my eyes. I eventually began a love affair with coding, especially with JavaScript, as I realized that this was something I would love to be doing for life.

When not coding, I spend my free time with family, playing MMORPGs, binge-watching Anime (yep, I'm a geek), running, and trying out new food.

I love AquaMan and strongly feel that he should be in more movies. :)
